How to Calculate How Much Epoxy For Your Table

Epoxy tables are a beautiful addition to any home. Calculating the right amount of epoxy is crucial for a perfect finish. Here’s how you can do it:

Step 1: Measure the length, width, and depth of the area to be filled.
Step 2: Multiply these values to get the volume.
Step 3: Convert the volume to liters or gallons as needed.

Always order a little extra to account for spillage and air bubbles. For more tips, consult with our professionals.
